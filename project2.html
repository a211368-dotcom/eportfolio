Problem Statement

Science learning often relies on manual note-taking and traditional classroom activities, making it difficult for students to organize experiment records, monitor learning progress, and collaborate with peers effectively.

Problem

  • Experiment notes are not centrally organized.
  • Students cannot easily track completed experiments.
  • Limited collaboration and knowledge sharing among students.
  • Lack of quick access to science-related educational resources.

Impact

  • Reduced learning engagement.
  • Difficulty revising experiment findings.
  • Loss of important experiment records.
  • Limited peer-to-peer learning opportunities.

Proposed Solution

  • Experiment Progress Tracking using Room Database.
  • Science Fact Integration through Wikipedia API.
  • Experiment Evidence Capture using Camera Sensor integration.
  • Community Discussion Board powered by Firebase Firestore.
  • Personal Learning Journal for recording experiment observations.
