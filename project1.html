Problem Statement:

Problem: Many students struggle to understand science experiments because learning is often based on theory without enough interactive activities.

Impact: The lack of interactive learning reduces student engagement and interest in science subjects.

Solution: LabQuest provides an interactive science learning platform that supports students through virtual experiments, quizzes, and learning materials aligned with SDG 4.
